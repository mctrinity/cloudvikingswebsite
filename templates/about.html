About Cloud Vikings

Cloud Vikings is more than just a Clash of Clans clan—we're a community of battle-hardened warriors who thrive on strategy and collaboration. Founded on February 13, 2016, by a group of passionate gamers, our clan has grown into one of the most competitive and respected forces in the game. But more than that, we are not just gamers—we're friends, bound by our shared love for the game and the camaraderie that comes with it.

We believe that victory comes through teamwork, dedication, and careful planning. Our members come from all over the world, uniting under one banner to conquer the battlefield. Whether it's clan wars, raids, or tournaments, Cloud Vikings always fights to win.

Join us and be part of something bigger—a community of friends who push each other to achieve greatness in every battle!
